Robo: DU-0

Du-0 is a Twin-Stick Shooter game, with the goal to reach the end of level quickly. The twin stick shoote control scheme is that two analog sticks or direction pads are used: one to control player-character motion, and the other to control the aiming of a ranged weapon. Players can control the character's movement, aim a ranged weapon, and attack with a melee weapon, all independently. The player character will be a cute robot, with an early conceptual drawing depicting a spherical head attached to an oval body and a floating gun in detached hands.
